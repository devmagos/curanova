Three-Phase Approach

From Pilot to Full Autonomous Operation

Curanova's deployment model is designed to prove value fast and scale without disruption. You see returns within the first growing season.

01

Foundation

Sensor Deployment & Baseline (Months 1–4)

  • IoT soil and micro-climate sensor network installed across the farm
  • Historical field data digitised and ingested into the AI platform
  • Agronomist-validated AI crop monitoring model trained on your crops
  • Farm team trained on the dashboard and alert system
  • Baseline yield and input benchmarks established
02

Integration

Robot Fleet Deployment (Months 5–10)

  • Robot fleet delivered, configured, and field-calibrated by Curanova engineers
  • Autonomous weeding, spraying, and monitoring begins in high-priority zones
  • Existing tractors and equipment integrated via controller APIs
  • Real-time telemetry dashboard live for farm manager
  • First productivity and input saving reports delivered
03

Scale

Expansion & Advanced Analytics (Month 11+)

  • Fleet expanded to full farm coverage — every field zone autonomous
  • AI yield prediction models achieving sub-5% error rates
  • Variable-rate input prescription maps active at sub-metre resolution
  • ESG and carbon-offset reporting dashboard available
  • Multi-farm co-operative integration for shared fleet management
