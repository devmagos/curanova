The Crisis

Agriculture Is Breaking Under Structural Pressure

Commercial farming is confronting chronic labour shortages, accelerating climate volatility, and soaring input costs — all while global food demand rises relentlessly. The 20th-century model of human-operated heavy machinery cannot meet a 21st-century food security challenge.

  • Global food production must increase 70% by 2050 to feed 9.7 billion people
  • Average farm operator age has reached 57 years — a workforce ageing out
  • Up to 40% of crops lost in Sub-Saharan Africa due to post-harvest inefficiency
  • Agriculture accounts for 70% of global freshwater withdrawals
